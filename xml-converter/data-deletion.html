What data we collect

This app does not collect or store any personal data on its own servers. It does not require registration or login. Files you open are processed entirely on your device and never uploaded.

The third-party advertising SDK (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers and advertising interaction data for ad delivery and analytics. This data is processed by Google, not stored by us.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us at: andrsukhanov@gmail.com
  2. Include the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. We will process your request within 30 days.

For advertising data managed by Google AdMob, you can also reset or delete your advertising ID directly in your device settings:
Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset/Delete advertising ID.

Data retention

We do not retain personal data. App preferences (theme, language, Pro status) live only on your device and are removed when you uninstall the app. Advertising identifiers are managed by Google per their privacy policy.
